How to get accurate results

✅
Include quantities
"2 cups of rice" is much better than just "rice"
✅
Describe cooking method
"fried chicken" vs "grilled chicken" changes calories significantly
✅
You can log multiple items
"1 egg, 2 slices bread, and 1 cup tea" works perfectly
💡 Rwanda tip: Use foods like ugali, isombe, ikivuguto, and ibirayi. The Edamam API recognizes most common foods by their English names.
